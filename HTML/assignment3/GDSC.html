GDSC

Developer Student Clubs (DSC) are community groups for college and university students interested in Google developer technologies. We are a community of passionate developers and technologists dedicated to helping students build their skills, create innovative projects, and connect with like-minded peers.

WHAT DO WE DO?

Creating a Collaborative Community - The club promotes a collaborative environment, peer mentoring, and networking through coding meetups and hackathons.

Skill Development and Training - We organize workshops on web development, app development, machine learning, and cloud technologies.

Driving Innovation and Projects - By hosting hackathons and supporting project incubation, DSC encourages students to develop impactful tech solutions.

Industry Exposure - With guest lectures and tech talks, DSC prepares students for industry by connecting them with professionals.
